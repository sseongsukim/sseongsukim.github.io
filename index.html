Research

My research goal is create robots capable of naturally blending with humans and solving complex tasks in the real-world. Specifically, I am interested in training challenging robots, such as humanoids and dexterous manipulators.

To achieve these goals, I focus on offline RL by 1) using weakly-labeled or unlabeled datasets to enhance data-efficiency, and 2) using human teleoperator to train human-like behaviors.
