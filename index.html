Seongsu Kim

Hi, I'm Seongsu Kim! I am a researcher at Korea Electronics Technology Institute (KETI). My current work centers on developing imitation learning and reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms for dual-arm manipulation with multifingered hands, precision insertion using visual-tactile sensing, and whole-body control for quadruped and humanoid robots.

I received my Master's Degree from Hanyang University, advised by Jun Moon, where I worked on imitation learning, offline RL, and offline goal-conditioned RL.

My research goal is to develop robot learning algorithms that efficiently leverage datasets to enable robots to acquire dexterous skills and generalize across diverse tasks and environments. Toward this goal, I am interested in offline RL, offline unsupervised RL, imitation learning with generative models, and world models.
